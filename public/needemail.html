Why do we need your Email?

Once you have completed the registration form, you will be sent a confirmation e-mail detailing your username and password information.

Your email address is your key identifier to your account with SwapMyWhip.com.
SwapMyWhip.com respects your privacy, and your email address will not be rented, distributed, or sold to any outside parties. SwapMyWhip.com may occasionally send you information relevant to your listing (such as a reminder that your listing is due to expire.) Read our Privacy Policy for more information on this.
